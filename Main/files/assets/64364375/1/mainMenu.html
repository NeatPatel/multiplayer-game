How to Play

Dominationz.io is a king of the hill where you get points! Try to knock people off the center, or even break open chests (coming soon)! The middle gives the most points, so be sure to bombard the center with all your might! Good luck Dominator!!!!

Controls

Movement: W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys

Look: Mouse

Use Weapon: Mouse Left Click

Swap Left Inventory Item: Q

Swap Right Inventory Item: E

Dash: SPACE

Open shop: B

Sell Currently Held Item: G

Chat: Enter

Open/close menu: Esc
